Gungirl Sequencer

Gungirl Sequencer is an easy to use Audio Sequencer, primarily for, but not limited to the Linux Operating System. It features a timeline with eight tracks and a small integrated Filemanager. You can use Drag and Drop to arrange Audio Samples.
